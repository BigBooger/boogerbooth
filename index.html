Who Even Are You

I'm Booger and I'm awesome. I go by other names like AidsBooger, PoopBooger, and BoogerDude. I only use those ones because the username "Booger" is taken on most platforms though.

What Is Booger Booth

Booger Booth is just for whatever I feel like putting on it. I don't really have all that many plans for this website other than using it as some kind of personal portfolio or something. I'll probably upload stuff that I wanna keep so I can look back on it later like screenshots from games and random doodles. I'm getting back into web development after like 2 years so I'm a little bit rusty and this site is very scuffed at the moment.
